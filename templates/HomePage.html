Who Are We?

小蛙塘 is a student-run brand that lets kids learn microeconomics by selling handmade products. We mix fun, creativity, and a touch of professionalism to make learning exciting for children and their parents.

What We’re All About?

At 小蛙塘, we teach kids how to sell and promote handmade products while introducing them to basic economics. It’s all about hands-on learning, creativity, and having fun while discovering how the world of business works!

Our Fun & Fresh Look

Our style is a perfect mix of playful and polished! With clean, simple designs for our promotional materials, we make sure Xiaowadang looks fun and professional at the same time. Think of it as the best of both worlds!

Learning Made Fun

We believe in learning by doing! Kids get to sell products, tell stories to promote them, and work together in groups. With guidance from teachers, they gain real-world skills in business, communication, and teamwork—all while having a blast.
